Now that it's gone I can share a little secret: for 4 years the whole *.futureofcoding.org set of services (Newsletter, History, Search, Links, People) was running in a tmux session (I call it end-user sysadmin)

Now they are running as systemd services, that means I can restart the server without having to manually recreate the tmux session.
