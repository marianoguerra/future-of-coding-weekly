Tudor Girba 2021-09-17 19:14:09

Thinking of a generic visualization can be interesting, but I would suggest to consider starting from your context and the hypotheses you already have.

Chances are that you rely on one or more frameworks, some of which might be custom made. Maybe you even have naming conventions for different processes or queues. To get value from the analysis, you will want to start from those and walk your way up.
